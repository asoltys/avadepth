With the Soundings you can review the soundings in effect on a given date.

  • Enter a date. click on the date text to choose using a calendar.
  • Click on the Soundings button.

Avadepth displays the soundings report. To print a copy to your printer:

  • In your browser menu, you can select File|Print to print a hard-copy.

To display a riverbed profile:

  • click on one of the graph tabs to display the riverbed profilea graph showing the soundings along the length of the navigation channel compared to the nominal profile.
  • To print a hard-copy, select click File|Print menu item.

More Information

Channel soundings vary from time to time due to the effects of sedimentation, scour and dredging operations. Avadepth maintains a time-series database of minimum soundings for every one-kilometre interval along the channel, based on historical survey data, and automatically selects the soundings data in effect on any date specified by the user.
