Help - Currents & Velocities

The Currents and Velocities displayed are computed using the Canadian Coast Guards 2 Dimensional Model. The arrows size colour and represent speed as well as direction.

To predict currents and velocities for a specific date either past or present.

  • Click on the date.
  • Enter a date or click on the Date text to select a date using a calender
  • Click on the Next button

Avadepth displays the options to use a flow rate. These are predicted, actual, user-defined or select from the list.

  • Select the flow rate to be used.

To select the time you would like the Velocities and currents to be reported.

  • Select an hour and minute from the drop down boxes.

To select which zone you want to predict currents and velocities for.

  • Select the zone number from the drop down box or click on box in the map to the right.
  • If the map doesn't appear click on the Zone: text.

Depending on the discharge you may want to have a higher or lower maximum velocity.

  • Select the maximum velecity (2m/s or 4 m/s) using the radio buttons.

To print the picture to your printer:

  • In your browser menu, you can select File|Print to print a hard-copy.
