The available depth information provided in this report is compiled using the most recent hydrographic survey data of the Fraser River obtained by Public Works Canada and water levels provided by the Institute of Ocean Sciences, Fisheries and Oceans Canada. The Hope discharge is a forecast for each day. Variations between the actual and forecasted discharge rate will cause variations in available depths particularly when flows exceed 4,200 cubic metres per second.
