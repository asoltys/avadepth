Help - Water Levels

The water levels reported are computed using the Canadian Coast Guards 1 Dimensional Model. The water level value represents a single point every two kilometres on the navigation channel centreline.

The water levels are relative to local low water levels.

To predict the water level for a specific date either past or present.

  • Click on the date.
  • Enter a date or click on the Date text to select a date using a calender
  • Click on the Next button

Avadepth displays the options to use a flow rate. These are predicted, actual, user-defined or select from the list.

  • Select the flow rate to be used.
  • Click on the report to display the water level matrix.

To display a graph of water levels from Km 0 to Km 40 at a particular time.

  • Click on a time in the Time Column in the Water Level Report

To display water levels at a single Km site for the entire day.

  • Click on a Km on the Location SandHeads Km0 to Port Mann km 40 Title

To print the graph to your printer:

  • In your browser menu, you can select File|Print to print a hard-copy.
